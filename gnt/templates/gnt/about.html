About Bartensor

Welcome to Bartensor.
This website is a capstone project made by four students at The Ohio State University. Bartensor uses IBM Watson's services to become your perfect bartender assistant. Whether you're hosting an affair, or drinking alone, ask and you shall receive drink recommendations, recipes, and tantalizing chit-chat with our intelligent and suave bartender.

Bartensor can help you with simple tasks such as finding a recipe for a specific drink. It can also find drinks you can make given a list of ingrediants. We've also analyzed the flavor profile of drinks, so that you can filter to your picky heart's desire. By signing up for a Bartensor account, you gain the ability to like and dislike drinks, which influences Bartensor's recommendations for you.
